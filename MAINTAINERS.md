## Sponsoring Organization

The Construct is a project of **Fresh Pass Inc.**, a software firm. The project is maintained as open-source software and governed
by its community of contributors.

Sponsorship means the organization employs maintainers and funds the project's
infrastructure. It does not mean the organization controls the direction of the
project — architectural decisions follow the principles in [DECISIONS.md](DECISIONS.md)
and are made in the open.

---

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have made sustained, significant contributions to the project
may be invited to become maintainers. There is no formal application process —
the existing maintainers will reach out.

What sustained contribution looks like:
- Multiple merged pull requests across different areas of the codebase
- Active participation in issue triage and design discussions
- Demonstrated understanding of the project's principles and architecture
- Reliability — showing up consistently over time, not just in bursts

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review pull requests in your area within **5 business days**
- Triage new issues within **3 business days**
- Participate in architectural discussions when they affect your area
- Keep your contact information current in this file
- Follow and enforce the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
